About Me

I’m Joseph Chidubem Okafor, A Full Stack Software Engineer, Electronic Engineer, and Digital Strategist passionate about building impactful, people-centered solutions. I hold a B.Eng. in Electronic Engineering from the University of Nigeria Nsukka (UNN), and my journey spans both hardware and software systems. With hands-on experience as an Electrical Engineer at Evergreen Technology and as a Solutions Architect at CAP Nigeria, I bring a unique blend of engineering precision and software innovation. I’ve been professionally trained through ALX, 3MTT, and IHS, specializing in Full Stack Development, DevOps, and Generative AI. As the founder of Dubicventures, I lead the development of scalable digital platforms including the HIPAA-compliant CareCycle Hospital Management System, built with Django and MySQL. I’ve optimized user experience by 15% at PACUSS, managed technical infrastructure, and led strategic campaigns with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and human connection.
