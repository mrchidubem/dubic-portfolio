About Me

I’m Chidubem Okafor, a passionate and multi-skilled Electrical Technician, Software Engineer, and Digital Strategist with a mission to create impact through smart technology and meaningful human connection. With a background in Electronic Engineering from the University of Nigeria, hands-on experience as a Facility Technician at CAP Nigeria, and former work as an Electrical Contractor at Evergreen Technology, I’ve built a solid foundation in system design, troubleshooting, and infrastructure optimization. But my journey didn’t stop there. I immersed myself in software engineering through rigorous training at ALX, earning certifications from Microsoft and IBM in Full Stack Development, DevOps, and Generative AI. This unique mix of hardware and software expertise empowers me to solve real-world problems from the ground up — literally. I’m also the visionary behind Dubicventures, a startup idea focused on building scalable digital solutions. One of my flagship projects is the CareCycle Hospital Management System — a HIPAA-compliant platform built with Django and MySQL, designed to revolutionize healthcare management through modular, secure, and user-friendly interfaces. My portfolio isn’t just technical — I’ve worked in beauty store management, coffee shop operations, and social campaigns that support YouTube growth and Google Reviews engagement. I’ve developed systems where users are rewarded for digital participation, generating income while supporting businesses. I’m driven by values like honesty, loyalty, emotional depth, and excellence — and I bring those to every relationship, project, and opportunity. I believe in resilience through purpose and that every setback is a setup for something greater. Whether I’m building robust backend systems, deploying clean front-end interfaces, or managing operational workflows, I work with a single mission in mind: To create solutions that work, connect people, and leave a legacy.
